FAQs

What time does the lunch menu start and end?
Our lunch-specials are served from the time we open till 3pm daily.
Do you serve buffets?
No, we only have deliver take-outs and dine-ins.
Can we make reservations?
Yes! A minimum of 6 people can make a reservation at any one of our 2 VIP rooms.
Do you provide T.V.s and Wifi?
Yes we have TVs as well as Wifi to see to your comfort and satisfaction.
Does your food contain MSG?
Some of our items do contain MSG. For those who insist on not consuming, can simply make a request to our stewards who would be most willing to see to its removal. This also applies to other special requests with regards any allergies that you may have. Your satisfaction is our top goal!
